Pool Rules
📋 Format
Each entry picks 8 golfers across 4 tiers:
  • Tier 1 — Favorites · Pick 3
  • Tier 2 — Contenders · Pick 2
  • Tier 3 — Dark Horses · Pick 2
  • Tier 4 — Longshots · Pick 1
You cannot pick the same golfer more than once.
🏌️ Daily Scoring
Each day, your best 4 scores of your 8 golfers count. The 4 worst are dropped. Your final score is the sum of your best-4 daily totals across all four rounds. Lowest after Sunday wins.
✂️ The Cut
Golfers who miss the cut automatically score +10 for Saturday and Sunday. Those scores still count toward your daily calculation — but they'll hurt. Strategy tip: having reliable cut-makers matters.
🎯 Tiebreaker
Ties broken by closest prediction to the winning score to par. You submit this with your picks.
🔒 Picks & Privacy
Picks lock at Thursday April 9, 9:00 AM ET. You can update your picks any time before then. Other people's picks are hidden until the tournament starts. After lock, everyone's full picks are visible on the leaderboard.
